Your Questions Answered!

Will I get a certificate?

Burntune will not only certify your learning but will also help you create and launch your work to the world!

How long will it take me to learn music properly?

Burntune assures you of being able to play and sing resobaly well anywhere between 3 months to 1 year, based on your practice and performance.

What can I do once I learn how to play?

Burntune would love for you to be able teach on our platform and even help you write and share your music to fans who will love you.

Can I learn advanced Music at Burntune?

Burntune makes it easy for beginners and even intermediate players to sharpen their skills not only in being able to play music but use it create your own independent work.

Are there any offline lessons available?

Burntune is making an impact globally as a result of which we teach Music online. As soon as the Pandemic relaxes, we will soon be creating centers  in major citites in India to be able to reach you.

Can International students learn from Burntune?

Absolutely. We communicate in English and make learning equally fun for learners from all around the world.
